Vocabulary of English Speaking Children

Age:

Using the vocabulary dataset from Stanford University's Wordbank research team, we created an interactive word cloud with the vocabulary understood and used by English-speaking children aged 16 months to 29 months. Users can click on the Click Me! button to see how the range of vocabulary changes as kids grow older. We used and modified Jason Davies' word cloud package on D3.
